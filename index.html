Discover Speed Coaching & Your Event Experience

This event is your gateway to understanding coaching. We aim to demystify what coaching conversations are, show you its benefits first-hand, and spark inspiration for your personal and professional growth. It's more than an event; it's an experience designed to enlighten and empower.

Event Highlights:

  • Audience
    Individuals keen on personal/professional development and curious about coaching.
  • Live VC Demos
    See live coaching conversations with 3 participants, demonstrating the power of making a shift within just 10-minutes with coaching support.
  • Explore What Matters to You
    Explore a topic you've been avoiding or may benefit from some safe space to process.
  • Attendance (30 max)
    To maintain the quality of conversation and connection, attendance is thoughtfully capped at 30 participants. We kindly ask that you register only if you're able to participate fully, so we can honor the space and opportunity for everyone.
  • Connection
    Connect with others who are facing similar challenges.

Why You Should Attend

Participating in this Speed Coaching event offers tangible benefits that can catalyze your growth. You'll not just learn about coaching; you'll live it, feel its impact, and leave with actionable insights.

Move beyond theory. Engage in real coaching conversations and directly understand how this powerful tool can help unlock your potential and navigate challenges in your life.

Demystify what coaching truly is (and isn't!). Understand the nature of coaching conversations, their focus on forward movement, and how they differ from therapy or mentoring.

Leave with a renewed sense of purpose and a spark to reflect on areas you wish to grow into. Identify actionable steps to begin your journey of development immediately.

What happens post event?

This session is just the beginning — you’ll receive a short follow-up with actionable steps. These are a sneak peek at what's possible to continue your growth journey:

Deepen your exploration with a complimentary sample coaching session with one of our coaches. A great way to discuss your specific goals.

Spread the word! Share what you learned and experienced with 2-3 other people. Sharing reinforces your learning and might inspire others.

Connect with a fellow participant to become accountability buddies. Set a timeline to check in on your progress and support each other.

What one thing will you do differently on Monday as a result of this event? Define it, commit to it, and tell 2-3 people to solidify your intention.
